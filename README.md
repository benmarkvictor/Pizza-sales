# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive **Pizza Sales Dashboard** developed in Microsoft Excel to provide valuable insights into business performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product sales trends. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful visualizations that help business owners and decision-makers monitor sales performance, identify profitable products, and understand customer demand patterns.

By combining key performance indicators (KPIs), interactive charts, and dynamic filters, this dashboard enables users to explore sales data efficiently and make informed, data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance using key sales metrics.
- Analyze total sales, average sales, orders, and quantity sold.
- Identify the best and worst-selling pizzas.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior by day and time.
- Compare revenue generated by different pizza categories and sizes.
- Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting.

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 🍕 Revenue by Pizza Category

The dashboard reveals that **Classic Pizza** generated the highest revenue, making it the strongest-performing product category. Chicken Pizza closely followed, demonstrating consistently high customer demand. Supreme and Veggie pizzas also contributed significantly, indicating a well-balanced product portfolio.

---

### 🏆 Best-Selling Pizzas

The top-performing pizzas include:

- The Thai Chicken Pizza
- The Barbecue Chicken Pizza
- The California Chicken Pizza

These products consistently generated the highest sales and represent the company's most valuable menu offerings.

---

### 📉 Lowest-Selling Pizzas

The least popular pizzas include:

- The Spinach Supreme Pizza
- The Green Garden Pizza
- The Brie Carre Pizza

These products generated considerably lower revenue compared to the best-selling menu items.

---

### 📅 Busiest Sales Days

Customer demand is highest on:

- Friday
- Saturday
- Thursday

Sales activity increases significantly toward the weekend, indicating stronger customer purchasing behavior during these periods.

---

### 🕒 Peak Ordering Time

The dashboard shows that customer orders peak around **12 PM (Lunch Time)** before gradually declining throughout the afternoon and evening. This insight helps businesses optimize staffing schedules and food preparation during busy hours.

---

### 🍕 Pizza Size Performance

Large-sized pizzas contribute the highest percentage of overall sales, followed by Medium and Small pizzas. This suggests customers generally prefer larger pizzas, likely due to better value for families and groups.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Increase Promotions for Best Sellers

Leverage the popularity of the Thai Chicken Pizza, Barbecue Chicken Pizza, and California Chicken Pizza by offering combo deals, meal bundles, and limited-time promotions to maximize revenue.

### Improve Underperforming Products

Review the pricing, ingredients, and presentation of low-performing pizzas such as the Spinach Supreme, Green Garden, and Brie Carre pizzas. Consider promotional discounts, recipe improvements, or menu optimization to increase demand.

### Prepare for Peak Business Hours

Since Fridays, Saturdays, and lunchtime experience the highest customer traffic, businesses should increase staffing levels and ensure sufficient inventory to improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

### Optimize Inventory Management

Use historical sales patterns to forecast ingredient demand accurately. Maintaining adequate stock for high-performing pizzas while reducing waste from low-demand products can improve profitability.

### Focus Marketing on High-Revenue Categories

Marketing campaigns should emphasize Classic and Chicken pizzas while introducing promotional strategies to increase awareness and sales of Veggie and Supreme pizzas.
